About me

I've been programming in Java for over 5 years now; everything I know has been self taught. I started to become interested in Java when I became more interested in how games where made and how people could modify a game to how they wanted. The most popular game at the time was Minecraft and luckily the modding community for it was huge.

It started with making small mods/tweaks for things that I wanted in the game. Whether it be a chat-TAB autocompleter or an auto item refiller, I started to expand my knowledge of Java. From there I found a couple of videos by a group called 'Team Avolition' who were notorious in the Minecraft community for Griefing (the process of annoying another player whether it be destroying their work or just generally being annoying).

Within their videos they can be seen using something called a 'Hacked client' which is basically an overall modification of the original Minecraft client to give yourself an advantage over the other players.
I started to research about this hacked client and then stumbled upon a forum called HackForums namely the griefing section. I quickly created my own hacked client and started a name for myself in this community, this is where Java for me blew up and I learned so much from spending time there learning from others in the community.

I have been programming in Java ever since, adapting it to many day-to-day uses (password creator, budget tracker, botting scripts and a message encrypter).
